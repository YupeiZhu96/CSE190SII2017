Also, there are more sophisticated ways to remove the visible watermarks. Those masters of Photoshops could remove the visible watermarks by hands. And recently, researchers of Google developed an new algorithm that can be used to remove the watermarks on pictures. It works astonishingly well.

The basic idea is learning from thousands of pictures that has the same watermark and computing out a close pattern of it. At last, do an inverse transformation on the pictures using the pattern. It works perfect when the pattern is fixed but its performance drops when the pattern is randomly-warped.
